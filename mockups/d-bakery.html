Arbitral findings can ground abuse-of-process arguments in later litigation

The Court of Appeal held that findings from a concluded arbitration may found a strike-out of subsequent proceedings under the extended doctrine of abuse of process, even absent strict issue estoppel.

Why it mattersRe-litigating points lost in arbitration now carries a live strike-out risk — plead around prior awards with care.
