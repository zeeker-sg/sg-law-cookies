Arbitral findings can ground abuse-of-process arguments in later litigation

In [2026] SGCA 31, the Court of Appeal held that findings from a concluded arbitration may found a strike-out of subsequent proceedings under the extended doctrine of abuse of process — even absent strict issue estoppel.

Why it mattersRe-litigating points lost in arbitration now carries a live strike-out risk. Plead around prior awards with care.
